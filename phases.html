The Eight Moon Phases

New Moon

The moon is between the Earth and Sun, invisible from Earth. Marks the start of a new cycle.

Waxing Crescent

A thin crescent appears, growing towards the first quarter.

First Quarter

Half the moon is visible, like a semicircle.

Waxing Gibbous

More than half visible, approaching full moon.

Full Moon

The entire moon is illuminated, brightest phase.

Waning Gibbous

More than half visible, shrinking towards last quarter.

Last Quarter

Half the moon is visible again, like a semicircle.

Waning Crescent

A thin crescent, fading to new moon.
